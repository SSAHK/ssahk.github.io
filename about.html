About Us

The Student Surgical Association of Hong Kong (SSAHK) is a student organization jointly run by medical students from the University of Hong Kong and the Chinese University of Hong Kong. It aims to broaden students’ horizons in different surgical specialties by connecting medical students from both medical schools with the surgeons.

We hope the establishment of the Association can provide medical students with opportunities from extra skills teaching to networking and socializing. Ultimately, we aim at encouraging surgical exposure, interest and education at an undergraduate level. And through organising events like career nights, we strive to provide a better communication platform between practising surgeons and medical students.

Our Missions

  • Inspire medical students from both the University of Hong Kong and the Chinese University of Hong Kong to pursue a surgical career by holding a variety of related activities and events
  • Expose students to a variety of surgical specialties in pre-clinical years
  • Promote and encourage communication between surgeons and medical students
  • Provide a social outlet for its members & students with an interest in surgery.
  • Expose students to a broader surgical community through overseas exchange and international symposiums

Honorary Advisors

Professor C.M. LO

Prof. C.M. Lo is the Chair Professor and Head of Department of Surgery at the University of Hong Kong. Professor Lo is internationally renowned for his innovation in hepatobiliary surgery and liver transplantation. He and his team pioneered adult living donor right lobe liver transplant since 1996 and revolutionized the world-wide practice of liver transplantation. By his surgical skills and innovation, he won China’s Top National Honours for Scientific Achievements in 2005.

Professor James Y.W. LAU

Prof. James Y.W. Lau is the Chairman of the Department of Surgery at the Chinese University of Hong Kong. He is the founder and chairman of the vascular surgery team at the Department of Surgery on his return from training in Edinburgh to serve the public patients. He is also the Chairman of the Research and Development in CUHK’s Jockey Club Minimally Invasive Surgical Skills Centre. Professor Lau specializes in endoscopic treatment, and has published over 100 papers in international medical journals, earning him the first holder of Yao Ling Sun Professorship in Surgery.
