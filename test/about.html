About

The Student Surgical Association of Hong Kong (SSAHK) is a student organization jointly run by medical students from the University of Hong Kong and the Chinese University of Hong Kong. It aims to broaden studentsâ€™ horizons in different surgical specialties by connecting medical students from both medical schools with the surgeons.

We hope the establishment of the Association can provide medical students with opportunities from extra skills teaching to networking and socializing. Ultimately, we aim at encouraging surgical exposure, interest and education at an undergraduate level. And through organising events like career nights, we strive to provide a better communication platform between practising surgeons and medical students.

Our Missions

  • Inspire medical students from both the University of Hong Kong and the Chinese University of Hong Kong to pursue a surgical career by holding a variety of related activities and events
  • Expose students to a variety of surgical specialties in pre-clinical years
  • Promote and encourage communication between surgeons and medical students
  • Provide a social outlet for its members & students with an interest in surgery.
  • Expose students to a broader surgical community through overseas exchange and international symposiums
