Advanced Surgical Workshop 2017

The basic suturing workshop will be held on 11 February 2017. It aims to teach more advanced surgical skills to medical students. Students will be able to practise knot tying and suturing skills on pig skin, dissection of pig skin, excision of artificial skin mass and closure of wound under the supervision of respected tutors.
