Suturing Workshops 2015

The suturing workshops, in Spring 2015, are introductory sessions on suturing and knot tying for students from both medical schools. Surgeons from non-teaching hospitals are invited to teach students on professional skills, and students can practice on their own sets of materials.

Through this learning experience, we hope that students are inspired by the skills and knowledge demonstrated by the surgeon, and glimpse into the life of surgeons.
