The 2017 Basic Suturing Workshop was successfully held on 4th (Sat) and 5th (Sun) of February at the Prince of Wales Hospital (PWH) and the Medical Campus of the University of Hong Kong (HKU) respectively. We hope that all participants would have had a wonderful learning experience into various knot-tying and suturing skills! Thank you all for coming.
